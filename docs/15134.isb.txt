kyaa bataa_uu.N ke mujhe tujhase mohabbat kyuu.N hai
mere mahabuub mujhe terii zuruurat kyuu.N hai

kashti\-e\-dil ke liye aur kinaare hai.n bahut
tere jalawo.n ke siwaa aur nazaare hai.n bahut
merii chaahat ke liye aur sahaare hai.n bahut

kyuu.N terii yaad sataatii hai mujhe shaam\-o\-sahar
jaane kyuu.N Dhuu.NDhatii\-phiratii hai tujhe merii nazar
hai.n zamaane me.n hasii.N aur bhii tujhase behatar
tuu hii batalaa ke mujhe tujhase mohabbat kyuu.N hai
