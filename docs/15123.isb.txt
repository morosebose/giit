Gazab kiyaa tere waade pe aitabaar kiyaa
tamaam raat qayaamat ka i.ntazaar kiyaa

% the following one sher is only in Rafi version
ha.Nsaa\-ha.Nsaa ke shab\-e\-wasl ashqabaar kiyaa
tasaliyaa.N mujhe de\-de ke beqaraar kiyaa

ham aise mahav\-e\-nazaaraa na the jo hosh aataa
magar tumhaare taGaaful ne hoshiyaar kiyaa

% the following one sher is only in Rafi version
fasaanaa\-e\-shab\-e\-Gam unako ek kahaanii thii
kuchh aitabaar kiyaa kuchh na aitabaar kiyaa

% Mehdi Hasan sang this ghazal with the following shers in addition 

tujhe to waadaa\-e\-diidaar hamase karanaa thaa
ye kyaa kiyaa ke jahaa.N ko ummiidavaar kiyaa

ye dil ko taab kahaa.N hai ke ho maan andesh
unho.n ne waadaa kiyaa hamane aitabaar kiyaa

na puuchh dil kii haqiiqat magar ye kahate hai.n
wo beqaraar rahe jisane beqaraar kiyaa
