hai apanaa dil to aavaaraa, na jaane kis pe aayegaa

hasiino.n ne bulaayaa, gale se bhii lagaayaa
bahut samajhaayaa, yahii na samajhaa
bahut bholaa hai bechaaraa, na jaane kis pe aayegaa
hai apanaa dil to aavaaraa \threedots

ajab hai diivaanaa, na ghar naa Thikaanaa
zamii.n se begaanaa, phalak se judaa
ye ek TuuTaa huaa taaraa,  na jaane kis pe aayegaa
hai apanaa dil to aavaaraa \threedots

zamaanaa dekhaa saaraa, hai sab kaa sahaaraa
ye dil hii hamaaraa, huaa na kisii kaa
safar me.n hai ye ba.njaaraa,  na jaane kis pe aayegaa
hai apanaa dil to aavaaraa \threedots

huaa jo kabhii raazii, to milaa nahii.n kaazii
jahaa.N pe lagii baazii, vahii.n pe haaraa
zamaane bhar kaa naakaaraa,  na jaane kis pe aayegaa
hai apanaa dil to aavaaraa \threedots
