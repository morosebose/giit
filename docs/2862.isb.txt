Husne jaana idhar aa aaena hoon main tera
Main sawaroonga tujhe saare ghum de de mujhe
Bhigi palken na jhuka...aaena hoon main tera...
 
Kitne hi daagh uthaye tu ne
Mere din raat sajaye tu ne
Chum loon aa mein teri palkon ko
De doon ye umar teri zulfon ko
Leke aankhon ke diye muskura mere liye
Meri tasveere vafa...aaena hoon main tera...
 
Teri chahat hai ibadat meri
Dekhta rahta hoon surat teri
Ghar tere dum se hai mandir mera
Tu hai devi mein pujari tera
Sajde sau bar karoon...aa tujhe pyar karoon...
Meri aaghosh men aa...aaena hoon main tera...
Husne jaana idhar aa....
