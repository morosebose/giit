dil javaa.N hai aarazuu javaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii mehar_baa.N
kab talak ye hamase beruKhii
kab talak rahegii chup zubaa.N

ye kyaa Gazab hai dilarubaa hai apane aap se Khafaa
dharii rahegii har adaa jo dil kisii pe aa gayaa
Guruur-e-dil rahegaa phir kahaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...

vo husn kyaa jo ruuThake nigaah ko na pher le
vo ishq kyaa jo chhe.Dake na zulf ko bikher de
vo soz kyaa uThe na jo dhuaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...

mai.n jaanataa huu.N ai parii jhukii-jhukii nazar terii
uThii to haar jaayegii zaruur muskuraaegii
na chhup sakegii dil kii daastaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...

ajiib shai hai pyaar bhii ki jiit bhii hai haar bhii
nashaa bhii hai Khumaar bhii Khizaa.N bhii hai bahaar bhii
yahii hai do dilo.n kii daastaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...

Guruur hai na shaan hai har ek adaa zubaan hai
yah kaisaa imtahaan hai ki husn mehar_baan hai
zamii.n pe jhuk rahaa hai aasamaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...

dabii-dabii jo aag thii kisii kaa pyaar ban ga_ii
mai.n aa ga_ii vahii.n hai dil jahaa.N
ek nazar idhar bhii ...
