bahaare.n bech Daalii, phuu.Nk Daalaa aashiyaane ko
zaraa sii raakh rakh dii, bevafaa tere dikhaane ko

ai pyaar terii duniyaa se ham bas itanii nishaanii leke chale
ek TuuTa hua dil saath rahaa, ek rotii javaanii leke chale

aa_e the baDe aramaan liye, siine me.n ka_ii tuufaan liye 
anjaam ye hai, siine me.n dabii ek Gam kii kahaanii leke chale 
ae pyaar terii duniyaa se ham   \threedots

do din kii muhabbat me.n aakhir, ham terii galii se kyaa lete
itanii bhii navaazish hai terii, aa.Nkho.n me.n ye paanii leke chale
ae pyaar terii duniyaa se ham   \threedots
