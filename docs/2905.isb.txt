    haay! aaj to mujh ko bahak jaane do

    haay! ik zaraa hosh me.n aa jaane do

    tum saa ik phuul hazaaro.n me.n nahii.n
    dil kii tasqiin nazaaro.n me.n nahii.n
    dil ke nazadiik zaraa aane do

    ek ho jaate hai.n begaane do
aa: itane chhalakaa ke na paimaane do
ta: aaj to mujh ko bahak jaane do

aa: din khushii kaa jo guzar jaayegaa
    qaafilaa dil kaa kidhar jaayegaa
ta: vaqt sharmaa ke Thahar jaayegaa
    apanii zulfe.n mujhe bikharaane do
aa: aaj to mujh ko bahak jaane do
ta: mast aa.Nkhe.n hai.n ki paimaane do
