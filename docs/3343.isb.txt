%           Yaman.

\printtitle
#indian
%
he naTawar giriidhaarii, muraliidhar banawaarii
mohe apane hii ra.ng me.n ra.ng le
he giriidhar gopaal, swaamii he giriidhar gopaal

apane ra.ng me.n aisaa ra.ng de
jo mere a.ng a.ng ko ra.ng de
ra.ng diyaa thaa jo raadhaa ko
mujh pe wo ra.ng Daal, he prabhu
he giriidhar gopaal  \threedots

terii liilaa nyaarii
shyaam muraarii, he banawaarii
diijo daras na.ndalaal, he prabhu
he giriidhar gopaal  \threedots

mujh ko man har, jag se kyaa Dar
tuu mere sa.ng sa.ng hai naTawar
he dukhabha.njan, he sukhakaarii
% a male voice joins shanta apte at this point, and
% the two voices sing together the rest of the song.
kar do ham ko nihaal, he prabhu

kar do ham ko nihaal
shyaam muraarii, he banawaarii
diijoo daras na.ndalaal, he prabhu
he giriidhar gopaal  \threedots
%
#endindian
\endsong
