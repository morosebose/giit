koI ye kaise bataa ye ke vo tanhaa kyo.n hai ? 
vo jo apanA thA vohii aur kisii kA kyo.n hai ? 
yahii duniyA hai to phir aisI ye duniyA kyo.n hai ? 
yahii hotA hai.n to aakhir yahii hotA kyo.n hai ? 

ek zaraa haath ba.Dhaa, de to paka.Dale daaman 
usake siine me.n samaa jAye hamaarii dha.Dakan 
itanI kurbat hai.n to phir faasalaa itanaa kyo.n hai ? 

dil\-e\-barabaad se nikalaa nahii.n ab tak koii 
ek luTe ghar pe diyaa karataa hai.n dastak koii 
aas jo TuuT gayii phir se ba.ndhaataa kyo.n hai ? 

tum masarrat kA kaho yA ise Gam kA rishtaa 
kahate hai.n pyaar kA rishtaa hai.n janam kA rishtaa 
hai.n janam kA jo ye rishtaa to badalataa kyo.n hai ?
