% Comment: Shakarrao Vyas used Vishnu Digambar Paluskar's, who was his guru, tune for the composition. The composition is poetically in the maatrik chhand Harigeetika which splits (2- 3-4 - 3-4 - 3-4 - 5); musically it is a Dhrupad, in raag Hameer, and could be the only Hindi film song which uses the percussion taal named 'tevaraa', found only in Dhrupad AFAIK. Tevra (tevaraa) has a 7-beat semantic, not surprising since the metre has a saptamaatrik base. Lata has sung it in 7-beat roopak taal. - Dhananjay Naniwadekar
