aa.Nchal me.n phuul chaa.Nd sitaare sajaa liye
naino.n me.n mai.nne aas ke diipak jalaa liye

sunate hii unakaa naam meraa dil dha.Dak uThaa
kajaraa lahak uThaa meraa gajaraa mahak uThaa
duniyaa ne dil ke bhed nigaaho.n se paa liye

karate hai.n sab hii pyaar magar is kadar nahii.n
ham unake kab huye ye hame.n Kud Kabar nahii.n
aa.Nkho.n ne nii.nd chho.D ke sapane sajaa liye

jaldii se i.ntazaar kaa mausam tamaam ho
jii chaahataa hai aaj ye jaldii se shaam ho
is beqaraar dil ko kahaa.N tak sambhaaliye

aa.Ngan me.n mere pyaar kii baaraat aayegii
thaa jisakaa i.ntazaar wahii raat aayegii
Kaabo.n me.n taajamahal wafaa ke banaa liye
