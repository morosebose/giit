bhiigii bhiigii fazaa san san san ke jiyaa
zulfe.n u.Daatii hai, Kushabuu churaatii hai
paagal cha.nchal mast havaa
bhiigii bhiigii fazaa san san san ke jiyaa ...

koyal ban me.n bole
nas nas me.n ras ghole
ko_ii naTakhaT jaise ghuu.NghaT chorii chorii khole
ko_ii aayaa nahii.n, bhaayaa nahii.n
kisakaa saayaa saath chalaa
bhiigii bhiigii fazaa \threedots

pal pal jho.nkaa aaye
aa.Nchal sarakaa jaaye
tan man meraa jiivan meraa dhiire dhiire gaaye
dekho dekho ha.Nsii, dil kii kalii
dil me.n aisaa dard uThaa
bhiigii bhiigii fazaa \threedots

ha.Nsate hai.n diivaane
banate hai.n afasaane
dil kii baate.n dil kii ghaate.n kaise ko_ii jaane
mujhe roko nahii.n, Toko nahii.n
mai.n ne rastaa Dhuu.NDh liyaa
bhiigii bhiigii fazaa \threedots
