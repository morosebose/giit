devataa tum ho meraa sahaaraa
mai.nne thaamaa hai daaman tumhaaraa -2
koras:
devataa tum ho ...

mubaarak:
thaam lo apanii raadhaa ko bhagavan
ruk na jaaye kahii.n dil kii dha.Dakan
ye na kahane lage koii birahan 
mu.Nh chhupaakar saa.Nvariyaa ne maaraa -2
